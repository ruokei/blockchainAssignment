Frequently Asked Questions

How blockchain helps with the music industry?

Some of the major challenges now afflicting the music industry can be solved by blockchain technology. Musicians can receive equal royalty payments, venues can prevent counterfeit tickets, and record labels can easily track music streams and quickly compensate artists who contributed to songs or albums .

What are peer-to-peer networks in Blockchain?

The blockchain technology that underpins it takes advantage of the power of peer-to-peer networks to create a shared and trusted ledger of transactions. Blockchain, as a distributed ledger technology, records transactions as an immutable timestamped digital block with senders and receivers identified. The blockchain networks are managed by no centralised authority, and only the members can validate transactions among themselves.

What are trying to solve?

Indie artists are usually faced with the following problems - strict rules, under-the-table transactions by third party companies and unfair distribution of revenues through royalty. We aim to achieve an environment where indie artists no longer have to worry about their rice bowls staying in the industry.
